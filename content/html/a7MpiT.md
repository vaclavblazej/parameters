    * page 9 : [boxicity]({{< base >}}html/a7MpiT) upper bounds [chordality]({{< base >}}html/fTqo40) by a linear function -- Lemma 4.15 ([8,11]). The boxicity $b$ upper-bounds the chordality $c$. We have $c \le b$.
