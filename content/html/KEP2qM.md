---# treedepth

abbr: td

tags: [vertex removal]({{< base >}}html/e26LM8)

equivalent to: [d-path-free]({{< base >}}html/s4EiWI)

providers: [ISGCI](https://www.graphclasses.org/classes/par_18.html), [PACE](https://pacechallenge.org/2020/)

**Definitions:**

1. Treedepth of a graph is height of an auxiliary rooted forest over graph's vertices such that all edges of the graph have ancestor-descendant relationship within the tree.
1. For a graph treedepth is 1 if the graph is a single vertex. Otherwise, it is the minimum value obtained by removing some vertex and taking maximum over treedepths of each connected component.
